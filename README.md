# Obsidian-Quran-Journal

I wanted a way to take notes on the Qur'aan while having each Ayah individually listed in both Arabic and English.

For my notes and studies in general, I use Obsidian, which is a great, free tool to take your own notes and store them as you wish. It utilises the markdown (`.md`) format to ensure that your notes stay simple, useful and preservable over the long term. Markdown is similar to the standard Text (`.txt`) files we are used to, but provides extra features for formatting. Both file types are openable on any devices that exists.

This repository contains full Markdown files for each Surah, with each Ayah having both the original Arabic and subsequent English translations. Simply import into your Obsidian Vault and start your studying.

## Features

- Each Ayah number acts as a link to the exact Ayah on `quran.com` for easy reference checking
- The translation used is the Muhsin Khan & Hilali one. Although some may be dissuaded from the translation due to how 'wordy' it is, it is the translation that is [recommended by many Scholars](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BDY8i9VQeZM) due to protections against erroneous mistranslations

Here is an example of the raw output of 2 verses from Surah al-Baqarah as an example:

```

---
#### [045](https://quran.com/2/45?translations=203)

> وَٱسْتَعِينُوا۟ بِٱلصَّبْرِ وَٱلصَّلَوٰةِ ۚ وَإِنَّهَا لَكَبِيرَةٌ إِلَّا عَلَى ٱلْخَٰشِعِينَ

> And seek help in patience and As-Salat (the prayer) and truly it is extremely heavy and hard except for Al-Khashi'un [i.e. the true believers in Allah - those who obey Allah with full submission, fear much from His Punishment, and believe in His Promise (Paradise, etc.) and in His Warnings (Hell, etc.)].

**Notes:**

- Another sample note

**Tags:** #another_test_tag

---
#### [046](https://quran.com/2/46?translations=203)

> ٱلَّذِينَ يَظُنُّونَ أَنَّهُم مُّلَٰقُوا۟ رَبِّهِمْ وَأَنَّهُمْ إِلَيْهِ رَٰجِعُونَ

> (They are those) who are certain that they are going to meet their Lord, and that unto Him they are going to return.

**Notes:**

- Sample note

**Tags:** #test_tag
```

## Notes

- Requests for other translations will not be added at this point due to the previously mentioned reasons
- The heading for each Ayah Number doubles as a clickable link to [quran.com](quran.com) for the specific translation for the specific ayah you are looking at. This is done for easy reference checking at will
- If other translation languages are required for those whose preferred study language is not English, I can add them. Please make a new request under the 'Issues' tab

## Usage

1. Simply download the `.md` files from the `Markdown Files` folder and place them into your Obsidian Vault
2. Open Obsidian and study!
3. Remember to always double check scripts and translations
